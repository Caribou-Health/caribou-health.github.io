About Us

Because we Caribou-t you

We're on a mission to transform healthcare through compassionate technology

Our Mission

Caribou is a personalized wellness platform addressing critical gaps in Canada's healthcare system by transforming diagnoses into tailored, holistic health plans. We bridge the disconnect between clinical consultations and daily health management.

Why Holistic Health

Your health is more than just treating symptoms. Physical health affects mental well-being, nutrition affects energy, and exercise influences sleep. We integrate all aspects of care into one cohesive plan because better outcomes come from addressing the whole person.

Safety First

Medical safety is at our core. Caribou supports—never replaces—the guidance of healthcare professionals. We provide care support based on your clinician's recommendations with reminders, tracking, and encouragement to keep you on track.

Why Caribou?

The caribou is known for its incredible journeys across vast landscapes, supporting its herd through challenging terrain. Like these remarkable animals, we're here to support you through your health journey—every step of the way.

Founded with the belief that healthcare technology should feel personal, not clinical, Caribou Health brings warmth and encouragement to digital health management.
